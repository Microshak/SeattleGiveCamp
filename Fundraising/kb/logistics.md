---
title: Event Logistics
category: logistics
---

Seattle GiveCamp is typically held annually in the late summer or early fall. The event runs for a full weekend (Friday evening through Sunday afternoon).

**Location:** The event is hosted at a venue in the Seattle area. Specific venue details are announced approximately 6-8 weeks before the event date.

**What to expect:**
- Friday evening: Kickoff, team formation, and project introductions
- Saturday: Full day of coding, with meals provided
- Sunday: Final presentations and demo showcase

**Parking:** Free parking is available at the venue. Details are shared with registered participants before the event.

**Meals:** Breakfast, lunch, and dinner are provided on Saturday. Breakfast and lunch are provided on Sunday. Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.) are accommodated — please let us know in advance.

**Tech stack:** Teams use whatever technologies best fit their nonprofit project. Common stacks include: Python/Django, JavaScript/React, C#/.NET, Ruby on Rails, and WordPress. Bring your own laptop and any development tools you prefer.

**WiFi:** High-speed WiFi is provided at the venue.

For specific questions about an upcoming event, please email michael@seattlegivecamp.org.
