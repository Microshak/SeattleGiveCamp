Seattle GiveCamp brings together volunteer technologists — developers, designers, project managers, and IT professionals — to build software solutions for local nonprofits over a single weekend.

**Who can volunteer?** Anyone with technology skills! We need:
- Software developers (all experience levels welcome)
- UI/UX designers
- Project managers
- Database administrators
- DevOps engineers
- Technical writers
- General IT support

**What to bring:**
- Your own laptop and charger
- Any development tools or software you prefer
- A willingness to collaborate and learn

**Time commitment:** The full event runs Friday evening through Sunday afternoon. Volunteers are expected to attend the entire weekend, but we understand if circumstances arise.

**Volunteer registration:** Sign up through the Seattle GiveCamp website. Registration typically opens 8-10 weeks before the event.

**Code of conduct:** All volunteers must follow our code of conduct, which promotes a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

If you need to cancel your volunteer registration, please let us know as soon as possible so we can adjust team assignments. Email michael@seattlegivecamp.org for registration changes.
