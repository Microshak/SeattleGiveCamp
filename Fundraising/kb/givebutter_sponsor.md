## Full Story / Campaign Details

### About Seattle GiveCamp

Seattle GiveCamp is an annual volunteer hackathon where technologists donate their time and skills to build software for Seattle-area nonprofits. Over one intensive weekend, teams of developers, designers, and project managers create websites, donation portals, member management systems, data dashboards, and more—then hand them off to the nonprofits at no cost.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization run entirely by volunteers. Every dollar we raise goes directly into making the event happen.

### What Your Support Covers

Sponsorship funds help us provide:

- **Meals** for 100+ volunteers across the weekend (breakfast, lunch, and dinner on Saturday; breakfast and lunch on Sunday)
- **Venue costs** to host the event in the Seattle area
- **Event supplies** including WiFi, signage, and materials
- **T-shirts** for volunteers and sponsors

Past GiveCamp projects have helped nonprofits save thousands of dollars in development costs, freeing up their budgets to focus on their missions. Your sponsorship doesn’t just support an event—it directly enables technology solutions that help nonprofits serve our community more effectively.
