**What is Seattle GiveCamp?**
Seattle GiveCamp is an annual volunteer hackathon where technologists build software solutions for Seattle-area nonprofits. It's part of the global GiveCamp movement.

**Who organizes it?**
Seattle GiveCamp is organized entirely by volunteers. The event is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization run by community leaders from the Seattle technology scene.

**How can my nonprofit apply?**
Nonprofit organizations in the Seattle area can apply to be a project recipient. Applications open approximately 3-4 months before the event. Selected nonprofits receive a custom software solution built by volunteer teams over the event weekend.

**What kind of projects are built?**
Projects vary widely based on each nonprofit's needs: websites, donation portals, member management systems, volunteer scheduling tools, data visualizations, and more.

**How can I help if I can't attend?**
You can sponsor the event, donate via our website, or help spread the word in your professional network. All support goes directly to making the event possible.

**Is this part of a national organization?**
GiveCamp is a grassroots movement. Each city's GiveCamp is independently organized by local volunteers. Seattle GiveCamp is not formally affiliated with other city GiveCamps, though we share the same mission and values.

For any other questions, contact michael@seattlegivecamp.org.
