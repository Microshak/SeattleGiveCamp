Seattle GiveCamp offers four corporate sponsorship tiers to support our annual nonprofit hackathon.

**Platinum — $5,000** (1 available)
- Premier logo placement on website and all event materials
- Dedicated recruiting table at the event
- Recognition in all social media and press releases
- Opportunity to give a 5-minute opening remarks
- Company logo on event T-shirts

**Gold — $2,500** (2 available)
- Logo on website and event materials
- Recruiting table at the event
- Recognition in social media and press releases
- Company logo on event T-shirts

**Silver — $1,000** (3 available)
- Logo on website and event materials
- Recognition in social media
- Company logo on event T-shirts

**Bronze — $500** (4 available)
- Logo on website
- Recognition in social media

Sponsorship directly supports: food and beverages for 100+ volunteers, venue costs, and event supplies. Every dollar goes toward creating technology solutions for Seattle-area nonprofits.

For sponsorship inquiries, please email michael@seattlegivecamp.org.
