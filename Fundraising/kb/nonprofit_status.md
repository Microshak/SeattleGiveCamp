---
title: Nonprofit Status & Tax Deductibility
category: nonprofit_status
---

Seattle GiveCamp is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

Our EIN (Tax ID) is available upon request. We can provide our IRS determination letter and W-9 form to any potential sponsor or donor.

As a 501(c)(3) organization:
- Corporate sponsorships may qualify as tax-deductible charitable contributions
- Sponsors receive a donation receipt for their records
- We are exempt from federal income tax under Section 501(c)(3)

For more information, please contact us at michael@seattlegivecamp.org to request our determination letter or W-9.
