“Seattle GiveCamp Sponsorship Opportunity”

“Helping Local Nonprofits Through Technology – Sponsorship Opportunity”

Technical details

“We’d love to explore a sponsorship partnership…”

Brand visibility at event
Community goodwill / CSR alignment
Engagement with local tech talent
Recognition in event materials

“Would you be open to a brief conversation?”
“Can I send over our sponsorship packet?”

Clearly secondary
